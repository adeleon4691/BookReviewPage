"The Martian" Book Summary

About the Book

In the not so distant future, a team of astronauts are on an exploration on Mars. Following a severe storm, "The Martian" follows the journey of astronaut Mark Watney, who was thought to be dead by his team and left on Mars. Mark faces many different problems as he tries to figure out how to survive on this desolate planet as well as how to get home.

Filled with action, real science, and a little humor here and there, this book is a fantastic book for anybody who loves a great survival story.

About the Author

"The Martian" was written by American author Andy Weir. "The Martian" was the first book written by Weir in 2011. Andy Weir grew up a huge fan of Science Fiction writing. He has a background as a computer programmer. He has written short stories as well as a few other novels with one being released in 2021.

    Novels by Andy Weir:
  • "The Martian" (2011)
  • "Artemis" (2017)
  • "Project Hail Mary" (upcoming 2021)

From Page to Screen

In 2015, the film for "The Martian" was released in theaters. The film starred Matt Damon portraying the character Mark Watney. The film was directed by Ridley Scott and has a 91% score on Rotten Tomatoes. The film does an excellent job of bringing the book to life. The movie is also able to show the science that Andy Weir talks about. From the scenes where Mark is trying to figure out how to grow crops on Mars, to the different teams trying to solve various mathematical equations that could help bring Mark home.
